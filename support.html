Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a Show?

Tap the + button in the tab bar and select "Create a Show," or go to your Profile, tap the Shows tab, and tap "+ Create Show." Give your show a title, description, and cover image.

How do I add episodes to my Show?

Open your Show and tap the + icon in the top right. You can upload a video directly from your camera roll or attach an existing post.

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password." Enter your email address and we'll send you a 6-digit code to reset your password.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Settings → and follow the account deletion steps. Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

How do I report a user or content?

Email us at shamanshogun@gmail.com with the username and a description of the issue. We take all reports seriously and respond promptly.

The app is crashing. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, delete and reinstall it — your account and data are saved in the cloud. If you're still having trouble, email us with your device model and OS version.
