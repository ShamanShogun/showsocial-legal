Child Safety Standards

ShowSocial's commitment to protecting children from exploitation and abuse.

Our Commitment

ShowSocial has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) content of any kind. We are committed to maintaining a safe platform and actively work to prevent, detect, and respond to any content or behavior that exploits or endangers minors.

Prohibited Content and Behavior

The following are strictly prohibited on ShowSocial:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) of any kind
  • Any content that sexualizes minors
  • Grooming, solicitation, or exploitation of minors
  • Any attempt to establish inappropriate contact with a minor
  • Sharing, distributing, or promoting CSAE content

Prevention Practices

  • Age requirements: ShowSocial requires users to be 13 years of age or older to create an account.
  • Content moderation: User-generated content is subject to our community guidelines, which prohibit all CSAE content.
  • In-app reporting: All users can report any post, video, or user profile directly within the app using the report feature available on every piece of content.
  • Account removal: Accounts found to be involved in CSAE activity are immediately and permanently banned.

Reporting Child Safety Concerns

If you encounter any content or behavior on ShowSocial that you believe involves child sexual abuse or exploitation, please report it immediately:

  • In-app: Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) on any post or profile and select "Report."
  • Email: showsocialapp@gmail.com — include as much detail as possible.

We review all reports promptly and take immediate action when violations are confirmed.

Reporting to Authorities

ShowSocial complies with all applicable child safety laws. In accordance with legal requirements, we report confirmed CSAM to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) CyberTipline and cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies.

Designated Safety Contact

For child safety inquiries, law enforcement requests, or to report CSAE content, contact our designated point of contact:

showsocialapp@gmail.com

We are prepared to speak about our CSAM prevention practices and compliance at any time.
